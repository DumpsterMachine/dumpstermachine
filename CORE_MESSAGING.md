# Dumpster Machine - Core Messaging

## Pain Points to Exploit
1. **Hidden Fees:** The industry is notorious for bait-and-switch pricing, weight overages, and surprise environmental fees.
2. **Unfriendly Service:** Grumpy dispatchers, unhelpful drivers, and poor communication.
3. **Delivery Issues:** Late drop-offs, missed pickups, and damaged driveways.

## Core Promises (The Antidote)
1. **Integrity:** We do what we say we're going to do.
2. **Great Service:** Friendly, communicative, and professional from booking to pickup.
3. **No Hidden Fees:** Transparent, flat-rate pricing. You know exactly what you'll pay.
4. **Straight Shooters:** No BS. Direct, honest communication.
5. **Professionalism:** Clean trucks, careful drivers (protecting the property), and reliable timelines.
